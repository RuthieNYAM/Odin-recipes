Coconut Rice

Popular in many parts of the world, coconut rice uses a slightly different method than you would normally use to cook rice. Typically, you would bring the water to a boil first before adding the rice, but in this recipe, you bring the milk, water, and rice all to a boil together.

Ingredients

  • 2 c. long grain rice, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (14-oz.) can coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 c. water
  • 1 tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • Toasted sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)
